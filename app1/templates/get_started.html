Technical Indicators

Technical indicators are mathematical calculations based on a stock's price and volume, used to predict future price movements. They help traders identify trends, momentum, and potential buy or sell opportunities. Our services leverage indicators like MACD, RSI, and EMA to provide actionable insights.

Our Trading Strategy

As a user, you'll benefit from a strategy that combines multiple technical indicators for reliable trading signals. We use the MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence) to identify momentum shifts, RSI (Relative Strength Index) to gauge overbought or oversold conditions, and EMA (Exponential Moving Average) crossovers to confirm trends. Additionally, we calculate support and resistance levels to set safe entry and exit points.
